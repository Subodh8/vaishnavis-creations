The Artist

Made by Hands.
Felt by Heart.

Hi, I'm Vaishnavi — a self-taught resin artist with a deep love for colour, texture, and the magic that happens when you pour your heart into something handmade.

It began with a single mould, a bottle of resin, and a handful of gold flakes. Today, every piece I create — whether it's a sparkling letter keychain or an elegant puja thali — is still made one at a time, with the same intention and care.

How Every Piece is Made

1
Designing the Mould

Every letter, shape, and thali starts with choosing the perfect mould and colour story.

2
Layering Resin & Gold Flakes

Resin is poured in careful layers — each embedding gold foil, glitter, or seashells by hand.

3
Hand-Finishing & Assembly

Sanding, polishing, adding gold-plated hardware — every detail is checked personally.

4
Packed with Love

Each order is wrapped and boxed with care, ready to be gifted or kept as a keepsake.
